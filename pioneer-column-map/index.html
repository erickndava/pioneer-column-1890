The Pioneer Column Route of 1890

  •  About The Map
  •  Disclaimer

This map is an attempt to spatially represent in detail, the route followed by the Pioneer Column - "...a force raised by Cecil Rhodes and his British South Africa Company in 1890 and used in his efforts to annexe the territory of Mashonaland, later part of Southern Rhodesia (now Zimbabwe)." Wikipedia

The major landmarks and route were derived from an account of the 'journey' by Horste as published in The Rhodesiana, Publication No. 12 September 1965

This navigable map is the Results portion a blog post - Anything for a Map...{Part 0}

The data provided on this site was collated from various sources and should not be taken as authoritative.

This map representation is mainly to be used for reference purposes only as the objective was to demonstrate the versatility of readily accessible web-mapping technologies, tools and techniques.
